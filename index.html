BACKGROUND

Many people opt for their own cars and vehicles instead of using Public Transport, leaving more congestion and pollution. They don’t feel convenient in traveling in public transport, because of various factors like wastage of time, problem of carrying spare coins, unethical behavior of staff and fellow passengers. Crime rates are increasing in the bus such as pickpocketing, eave teasing and moreover women don’t feel safe at night. People are playing unethical tricks like travelling in busses without tickets or not paying for it. Drivers are indulging in rash driving and are irregular since they aren’t following the stipulated schedule causing problem for passengers and wasting their valuable time.
